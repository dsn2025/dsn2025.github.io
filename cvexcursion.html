Walking Tour Napoli (extra social event)

Naples Certosa di San Martino & Pedamentina Hiking Tour

Duration: approx. 3 hrs

Meet your guide at the hotel, and embark on an unforgettable tour of the majestic Certosa di San Martino, the breathtaking views of the bay of Naples, descend on foot from a charming neighborhood to picturesque neighborhoods along the ancient Pedamentina pathway.

Reach Piazzetta Augusteo and start with the funicular up the Vomero hill. Reach the Belvedere di San Martino, the best vantage point observation of the city, and be amazed by the panoramic view it offers. Start your tour by learning about the history of the monastery (certosa) of San Martino and its transformation into a Neapolitan civic museum (1 h 15 min indoor guided tour).

Continue your tour by urban trekking (please, wear hiking shoes!!!) through the ancient route of the Pedamentina. The Pedamentina boasts 414 steps and dates back to the 14th century. Along this urban masterpiece, admire not only the breathtaking view of the Bay of Naples but also the beautiful monastery gardens.

To end your tour, stop at the famous local Pignasecca market, where you can immerse yourself in the flavors of Naples. You'll be able to sample foods (famous "pizza a portafoglio") from the stalls, delicatessens, bakeries, and pastry shops. Being the heart of Neapolitan street food, the Pignasecca market will not disappoint.

Return to hotel on your own.

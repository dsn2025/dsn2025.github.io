Registration

Authors should carefully read the policy for accepted papers.

You can register for DSN 2025 here (Please read the following information before making your booking.): COMING SOON!

  • Each accepted paper in any track must have a distinct full conference author registration. Each accepted workshop paper must have either a distinct workshop or a distinct full conference author registration
  • Please double-check the registration type and fee when placing your booking to ensure it matches the corresponding section listed in the table below.
  • Workshop only registration does not include banquet.
  • Extra tickets for the banquet can be purchased from the registration site for 100€/person.
  • Workshop registration gives access only to the corresponding workshop.
  • Early-bird registration deadline is by May 10, 2025. After this date, regular registration fees will apply.
  • Students that are not IEEE/IFIP member must provide proof (e.g., student's ID card, an official letter from the head of department/director of doctoral school providing the current status as enrolled student) of their student status to be eligible for student fee rate. Please, send the proof to dsn2025@critiware.com during the registration process.
  • All rates include VAT.

Registration deadlines

  • Author registration (all tracks except workshops): Apr 30, 2025
  • Author registration (only workshops): May 10, 2025
  • Non-author early-bird registration (all tracks): May 10, 2025

Cancellation policy

All refund/cancellation requests must be sent to dsn2025@critiware.com by May 31, 2025, AoE Time.
There will be an administrative fee of 50% for cancelled registrations.
You may only cancel your registration before the cancellation deadline.
Author registrations are non-refundable.

Policy for Accepted Papers

  1. Every paper has to have a full registration to appear in the conference proceedings.
  2. The conference is in person and papers are presented only in person.
  3. If none of the authors can come to the conference for any motivation the paper will be withdrawn and will not appear in the proceedings.
